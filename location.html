How to Find Us

Metro or Train

From the nearest suburban railway station or metro stop, take a short auto or taxi ride toward Cathedral Road. We are located on R.K. Salai near the Cathedral Road junction, a five‑minute drive from the main Mylapore market area.

Bus & Two-Wheeler

Multiple city buses stop at Mylapore and Cathedral Road; the restaurant is a brief walk from the nearest bus stop. Two‑wheeler parking is available behind the building.

Car & Parking

Limited on‑site parking is available. Additional paid parking can be found 150 meters east at Market Plaza. For weekend evenings, we recommend arriving earlier to secure a spot.

Accessibility & Amenities

  • Wheelchair accessible entrance & restroom
  • Highchairs and kids’ menu available
  • Service animals welcome
  • Allergen notice: We use nuts, dairy, and gluten. Please inform us of allergies.

Reservations & Takeout

  • Group bookings: For parties of 8+, please mention “private dining” for curated seating.
