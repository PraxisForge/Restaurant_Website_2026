From Mylapore to Your Table

Madras Masala began as a weekend experiment in a small home kitchen. Our founder, raised on the coastal spices and street-food stalls of Chennai, wanted to share the bright, layered tastes of home with friends and neighbors.

What started as a few dosa nights and biryani feasts quickly grew into a restaurant built on one simple idea: honest food that tastes like memory.

Recipes Rooted in Tradition

We celebrate the regional diversity of Tamil Nadu and the broader South Indian peninsula. Our menu blends time honored techniques slow roasted masalas, stone ground chutneys, and hand pressed dosas with seasonal produce sourced from local farmers.

"Every dish is balanced to highlight spice, texture, and aroma rather than heat alone."

The People Behind the Plates

Our head chef learned to cook at her grandmother’s elbow and trained in contemporary kitchens to refine those flavors for today’s palate.

The front-of-house team treats every guest like family, and our cooks take pride in preparing each plate by hand. We hire locally, train passionately, and celebrate the craft of cooking every day.

Community First

We partner with nearby farms and markets to reduce food miles and support small producers. Leftovers are minimized through careful planning and composting.

We also host monthly community dinners and spice workshops to share knowledge and keep culinary traditions alive.
